Welcome to the random character generator!

Do you have a hard time coming up with characters for a story or something else?
With this tool you will be able to generate random names and places of which the person is from.
Just write into the text area a range of how long the first names can be in letters.
For example: "4-6" will generate names with no more than 6 letters but no less than 4.
Not writing anything, or a faulty input will generate names with no limitation to the amount of letters.
